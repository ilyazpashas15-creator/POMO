What is Pomo?

Pomo is a customizable, AI-enhanced Pomodoro timer designed for modern workflows. Inspired by the legendary Pomodoro Technique—a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo—Pomo bridges the gap between scientific focus protocols and empathetic digital companionship.

How the Pomodoro Technique Works

The core philosophy is simple yet transformative: work with time, not against it. By breaking your day into intense 25-minute focus sprints separated by systematic short breaks, you prevent cognitive fatigue and maintain a high-performance "Flow State" throughout the day.
